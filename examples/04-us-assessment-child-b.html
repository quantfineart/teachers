Recommended Actions at Home

📖
Daily reading aloud (15-20 minutes) Have Child B read to you, a sibling, or even a pet. Focus on reading aloud — this builds fluency. Choose books they love. Don't correct every mistake — let them self-correct and enjoy the story.
🔢
Math facts practice (5 minutes daily) Multiplication tables through 10. Flashcards, apps, or quiz games — whatever they enjoy. Make it fun and fast, not stressful. They already love math — this just builds speed.
✏️
Low-pressure writing Encourage any kind of writing at home — journals, letters to family, shopping lists, captions on drawings, thank-you notes. The goal is getting comfortable putting words on paper, not perfection. Celebrate every attempt.
