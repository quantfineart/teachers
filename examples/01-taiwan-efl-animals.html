Animals Vocabulary

Year 2 English as a Foreign Language | 40 Minutes
Taiwan Year 2 (Age 7-8) EFL — Beginner 40 Minutes Interactive + Projectable

5 min Warm-Up: Animal Song

Sing "Old MacDonald Had a Farm" together. Hold up picture flashcards for each animal as it appears in the song.

Encourage students to make the animal sounds. It's silly and fun — that's the point.

先唱歌暖場，讓學生放鬆。每唱到一種動物時舉起對應的圖卡。

5 min Pair Practice

Students work in pairs. One student points to an animal picture, the other says the English word.

Then swap! Each student should say all 8 words at least once.

Walk around the room and listen. Praise every attempt — "Good job!", "Wow, you remembered!"

兩人一組練習。一個指圖片，另一個說英文。然後交換。走動聆聽，多多鼓勵。

5 min Review: Song Again!

Sing "Old MacDonald" one more time — but this time, students shout out the animal names in English instead of the teacher.

End with applause. They learned 8 new English words today!
