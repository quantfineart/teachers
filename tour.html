Getting Started

Three questions, then you're ready to go

The first time you open your workspace, your assistant will ask you three things. That's it.

1

Your name

So your assistant can address you personally — not "Dear Teacher."

"What's your name and what would you like me to call you?"
2

What you teach

Subject, age group, and country — so plans use your curriculum and your terminology.

"What country are you in and what do you teach?"
3

What you need right now

Your first task. No waiting — your assistant starts working immediately.

"What do you need help with right now?"
Shortcuts

Two shortcuts to start — grows as you do

Type these to go straight to the thing you need most. Your workspace can add more shortcuts over time, tailored to how you work.
